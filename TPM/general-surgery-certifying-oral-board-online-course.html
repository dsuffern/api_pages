Accessing Your Online Materials

When you register, you receive password-restricted access to an online dashboard that consolidates all your online course resources: streaming webinars of every course lecture, and a thick syllabus with written materials.

You will receive online access within one business day, and your written materials will ship via UPS at that same time.
+ How Long Do I Have Access To My Online Materials?
Online material and course content will be available for a minimum of one year following the administration of the associated exam. This allows you to start studying well in advance of your scheduled exam, and you have the option of upgrading to next year's live or home study course for a small additional investment. If your exam is more than one year out, call Customer Service at 877-225-8384 to extend your access.
+ This Guarantee Sounds Too Good To Be True—Is It For Real?
If you purchase a course with a 100% Money-Back Guarantee and do not pass your board exam, American Physician Institute will refund 100% of your tuition.
