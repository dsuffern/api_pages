MASTER MEDICAL UPDATE FOR THE
PRIMARY CARE PHYSICIAN


CLINICAL PEARLS IN PATIENT CARE

Our Master Medical Update for the Primary Care Physician conference recently took place in Orlando, FL. The event included an “All-Star” list of lecturers from a variety of medical specialties. For individuals that missed the event, a recorded version of the lectures is now available in our online course format.

Online Course Details

The online course enables you to view all lectures anytime, anywhere, and on any device. The core focus of the course is to provide you with up-to-date, practical medical guidance and clinical pearls in psychiatry, cardiology, dermatology, endocrinology, and neurology. The course is guided by Dr. Robert Sheeler of the Mayo Clinic, ensuring all information presented is relevant to the daily practice of primary care physicians. The course is presented by five specialists with deep knowledge of their areas of focus. Each faculty member equips you with authoritative knowledge of new medications and presents the very latest and most effective treatments in the following areas.

  • Psychiatry: Robert Doyle, MD - Harvard Medical
  • Cardiology: Matthew Sorrentino, MD - University of Chicago
  • Dermatology: Matthew Zirwas, MD - Ohio State University
  • Endocrinology: Wei An Lee, DO - University of Southern California
  • Neurology: Matthew McCoyd, MD - Loyola Medical

POWERFULLY PRACTICAL FOCUS

Since the focus of the course is practicality, you’ll be equipped with guidelines you can begin using right away, such as when to refer out, and how to address difficult patients with psychiatric, neurologic, cardiac, dermatologic, and endocrine-related conditions. Most importantly, you’ll discover how to translate this new knowledge into immediate improvements in your practice and patient care.

CME CREDIT

As part of your participation, you’ll earn 25 AMA PRA Category 1 CME Credits. Many employers, group practices, and training programs will reimburse you for the cost of Category 1 CME activity. So, be sure to ask!

Don’t miss out on this opportunity. Register now to view the online course.

Titles/Affiliations/Positions:
