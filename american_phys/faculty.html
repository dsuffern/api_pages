JOIN THE HUNDREDS OF PHYSICIANS WHO WORK WITH AMERICAN PHYSICIAN INSTITUTE EVERY YEAR

American Physician Institute provides physicians with the medical education resources necessary for preparing – and passing – their board examinations, as well being the complete go-to resource for those seeking Continuing Medical Education (CME) or Self-Assessment (SA) credits for Maintenance of Certification.

Each year, American Physician Institute works with hundreds of physicians to produce our top notch courses. The goal of each course is to provide high-value, up-to-date and comprehensive medical education to physicians from around the country -- to both help them expand their knowledge and improve the treatment they provide.

We are inviting you to join our faculty, to help us continue to produce the highest-quality content possible. Please watch our welcome message from Executive Director, Dr. Jack Krasuski, and take a look at a few of our course features and physician testimonials below.
