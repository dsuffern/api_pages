About Us

American Physician Institute provides high-yield continuing medical education, presented by knowledgeable experts and designed to be the most convenient and effective way to meet the career needs of practicing physicians. API courses and materials include:

  • Beat The Boards! – Psychiatry and Neurology Board Review Prep Courses specifically designed for ABPN certification. More info…
  • The Pass Machine – Board Review Prep Courses for a wide range of ABIM, ABS, ABEM, ABFM, ABP and other board certifications, More info…
  • MasterPsych.com – The complete career resource for the psychiatrist. More info…

API is located in the community of Oak Brook, Illinois. Although a part of the metro Chicago area, Oak Brook's park-like setting feels like an oasis of calm far removed from the urban bustle.
