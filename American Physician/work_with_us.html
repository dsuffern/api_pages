Join Our Growing Team to Help Produce High-Quality, Relevant Medical Education

Each year, American Physician Institute works with hundreds of physicians to produce dozens of board-preparation and CME courses. The goal of each course is to provide high-value, up-to-date and comprehensive medical education to physicians from around the country, to both help them expand their knowledge and to improve the treatment they provide.

We would like to invite you to join our faculty, to help us continue to produce the highest-quality content possible. We are currently looking for physicians to contribute to our medical education courses in the following capacities:

  • Lecturers
  • Medical Editors
  • Medical Writers
