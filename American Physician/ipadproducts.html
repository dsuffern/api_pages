How do I Purchase my iPad online using the Best Buy $500 e-Gift card?

Go to the Best Buy website at www.BestBuy.com and select “Create an Account” at the top right hand corner of the page. Create your account by entering the information requested, and press “Confirm.”

Search products using the search bar at the top of the screen. You may view the iPads available by searching “iPad” in the search bar. Press “Add to Cart” next to the iPad you wish to purchase.

Within your shopping cart, you may select “Store Pickup” or “Shipping.” If you choose “Store Pickup,” you will be prompted to enter your zip code and select “Find Nearest Stores.” “Select” your desired store and note the availability date for item pickup.

If you select “Shipping,” press “Checkout” to review your purchase and to enter your billing and shipping address. When you reach the “Payment Information” screen, scroll down and enter the card number and PIN provided in your gift card email in the “Gift Cards” section.
